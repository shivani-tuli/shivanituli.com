Projects

BioDot: Handheld CBC Device

Developed a point-of-care CBC device using microfluidics and dielectrophoretic separation to replicate large-scale CBC machinery. The handheld device (160mm x 100mm x 50mm) produces results in 2-4 minutes with 90-100% accuracy, designed for underserved healthcare settings.

Heardio: Smart Medical Pill Dispenser

Engineered a medical pill dispensing system with a smart bottle and mobile app for medication adherence. Features "one-dose" dispensing mechanism, auto-lock functionality, and fingerprint sensor security to ensure only the patient can access medication.

CPR Assist Device

Designed an assistive device for CPR training and real-world application that ensures proper compression depth and rate while reducing discomfort during administration.

Senior Design: ASD Self-Harm Prevention Sleeve

Designed protective sleeves for patients with Autism Spectrum Disorder to prevent self-injurious behavior. Device features dual Kevlar layers, neoprene/silicone middle layer, and cotton/spandex inner layer. Performed FDA Design Controls (21 CFR 820.30), FMEA, and V&V planning.
