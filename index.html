I am a Research Associate at the University of Pittsburgh and Harvard University. I earned my Master's degree (M.Eng) from Cornell University (2021-2022) and completed my undergraduate studies at the University of Pittsburgh in 2021, majoring in Bioengineering with minors in Economics, Industrial Engineering, and Chemistry.

My research integrates tissue engineering, microfluidics, and medical device development. I specialize in organ-on-chip platforms, 3D bioprinting, and drug delivery systems. I combine these interdisciplinary skills to develop physiologically relevant disease models and translational technologies for cancer research and cardiovascular medicine.

Outside the lab, I am passionate about healthcare accessibility and have organized breast cancer awareness campaigns and medical aid initiatives.
